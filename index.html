How it works

        

          Most palette generators for popular frameworks either match a single color or ignore the
          careful work that was put into creating the original palettes entirely. dittoTones
          analyzes the perceptual "DNA" (Lightness/Chroma curves) of systems like Tailwind or Radix
          and interpolates your target hue onto them.
